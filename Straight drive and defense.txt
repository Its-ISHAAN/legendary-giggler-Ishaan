    <h1>How to Play Straight Drive and Defence</h1>

    <section>
        <h2>Straight Drive</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Grip:</strong> Hold the bat with a relaxed but firm grip, both hands aligned properly.</li>
            <li><strong>Stance:</strong> Stand side-on with knees slightly bent and eyes on the bowler.</li>
            <li><strong>Backlift:</strong> Lift the bat in a straight line backward with the top hand controlling the lift.</li>
            <li><strong>Footwork:</strong> Step forward with your front foot toward the pitch of the ball.</li>
            <li><strong>Execution:</strong> Swing the bat down straight through the line of the ball, keeping the bat face vertical.</li>
            <li><strong>Follow-through:</strong> Continue the bat motion smoothly towards the bowler.</li>
        </ul>
    </section>

    <section>
        <h2>Defence</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Grip:</strong> Hold the bat firmly with both hands close together.</li>
            <li><strong>Stance:</strong> Keep your body relaxed but alert, with a solid base for balance.</li>
            <li><strong>Backlift:</strong> Keep it short and controlled.</li>
            <li><strong>Footwork:</strong> Move your foot to the pitch of the ball depending on its length.</li>
            <li><strong>Execution:</strong> Present a straight bat and gently block the ball with a soft grip to avoid giving a catch.</li>
            <li><strong>Head Position:</strong> Keep your head over the ball to maintain control and balance.</li>
        </ul>
    </section>
